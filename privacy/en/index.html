MedFinder Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Español Català English

MedFinder is an Android app for looking up official medicine information, CIMA/AEMPS package leaflets, and AI-assisted summaries and answers. This policy explains what data the app processes, why it is used, and how you can exercise your rights.

MedFinder does not replace professional medical advice. The app does not diagnose, treat or cure diseases. Always consult a doctor or pharmacist about health or medication decisions.

1. Controller and contact

Controller: Medix Solutions / MedFinder. Privacy contact: medixsolutions@proton.me.

2. Data the app may process

Use without an account

  • Medicine searches and functional data needed to answer the query.
  • Technical or anonymous identifiers for security, abuse prevention, AI cost control and aggregated telemetry.
  • Local device data such as preferences, local history, favourites or cache, depending on app usage.

Google account and synced features

  • Name, email address, Google user ID and profile photo if you sign in with Google.
  • Subscription or purchase status validated through Google Play Billing.
  • Favourites, notes, chat history or usage data when you use features that require account sync.

Camera

The app requests camera permission only to scan medicine barcodes. Camera images are processed to recognize the code and are not used for advertising or sale of data.

Medicine queries and possible health data

If you type questions about symptoms, treatments, pregnancy, breastfeeding, allergies or medication, you may include health-related data. Use these features only if you agree that your query is processed to generate the requested answer.

3. Purposes

  • Provide medicine search and official CIMA/AEMPS information.
  • Answer AI queries and generate informational summaries.
  • Manage account, session, favourites, notes, history and preferences.
  • Verify subscriptions, purchases and Premium access.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, malicious automation and excessive service usage.
  • Measure search quality, errors, performance and aggregated service usage.

4. Providers and third parties

We do not sell personal data. We may use service providers needed to operate the app:

  • Google: sign-in, Play Billing, Play Integrity, Play Console and Android services.
  • Cloudflare: backend infrastructure, Workers, databases, security and technical telemetry.
  • AEMPS/CIMA: official public source of medicine information.
  • AI providers configured by MedFinder to process queries and generate responses.

5. Retention and deletion

  • Local data can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • Account data is kept while the account is active or as needed for security, billing or legal compliance.
  • You may request or start account deletion from the app when available, or by contacting the privacy email.
  • Some technical records may be retained for a limited period for security, fraud prevention, audit or legal compliance.

6. Security

We apply reasonable security measures such as HTTPS/TLS, authentication, access controls, encrypted storage where appropriate, server-side validation and anti-abuse limits. No Internet-connected system can guarantee absolute security.

7. User rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection or portability of your data. Contact medixsolutions@proton.me.

8. Children

MedFinder is not directed to users under 18. If we identify data from a minor without an appropriate legal basis, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

9. Changes

We may update this policy to reflect legal, technical or functional changes. The current version will be available at this public URL.
